## Definition

**Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)** refers to a hypothetical AI system capable of performing any intellectual task that a human can perform, with equivalent or superior competence across all domains.

## AGI vs Narrow AI

Current AI systems — including large language models — are narrow AI: highly capable within specific domains but unable to generalize across tasks the way humans do.

## Current Research

Leading labs including OpenAI, DeepMind, and Anthropic are actively pursuing AGI-adjacent capabilities, though no consensus definition of "achieved AGI" exists in the research community.
